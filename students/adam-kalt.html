Biography

Originally from Westchester County, New York, I graduated from Boston College in 2005, and moved to Los Angeles, CA. I spent over 10 years in Los Angeles in the Fitness Industry, working in managerial roles for a number of gyms, eventually starting up my own Outdoor Fitness Business in 2008 (under the names Extreme Boot Camp South Bay and then Maximum Boot Camp). The Outdoor Business moved Indoors in 2013, becoming a Small Group Focused Indoor Training Studio - Kalt Fitness - which is still in operation today. In 2017, I decided to embark on a new challenge by pursuing a Career in Software Development, as it had always been something of great interest to me.
